About Ettarra Coffee House Supper Club

Welcome to Ettarra Coffee House Supper Club, where culinary art meets immersive dining experiences. We are not just a coffee house but a place where people come together to enjoy fine dining with a twist. Our club offers exclusive, themed supper nights that transport you to different cultures and tastes from around the globe.

At Ettarra, we believe in more than just great food. Our mission is to create unforgettable experiences that blend extraordinary cuisine with the warmth of community. Each month, we curate a different theme, featuring menus inspired by various cuisines, cultures, and seasons. Whether you are a food enthusiast, a lover of great company, or someone who appreciates unique dining settings, Ettarra Coffee House Supper Club is the place to be.

We also collaborate with renowned chefs and local artisans to bring you exclusive pop-up events, cooking classes, and wine tasting nights. Join us and become part of our family, where every meal is a celebration of flavors, friendship, and creativity.
